Dining at El Nido

Restaurants

Our resort features several on-site restaurants, each with its own unique menu and atmosphere. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a sit-down meal, you'll find something to please your palate.

The Seaside Grill

Located by the beach, the Seaside Grill offers fresh seafood, salads, and sandwiches. Enjoy a casual meal with a stunning ocean view.

The Garden Terrace

Our main restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner in a relaxed setting. Choose from a variety of international dishes and local specialties.

The Steakhouse

For a special night out, visit our steakhouse for prime cuts of beef, seafood, and an extensive wine list.

Bars and Lounges

After a day of exploring or relaxing on the beach, unwind at one of our bars or lounges. We offer a range of drinks and snacks, as well as live entertainment on select nights.

The Poolside Bar

Situated by the pool, this bar offers refreshing drinks and light bites. Take a swim and enjoy a cool beverage without leaving the water.

The Lobby Lounge

A stylish spot to meet up with friends or colleagues, the lobby lounge serves coffee, cocktails, and light fare.

In-Room Dining

Prefer to dine in the comfort of your room? Our in-room dining service is available 24/7, with a menu of appetizers, entrees, and desserts.

Special Events

Our resort also hosts special dining events throughout the year, such as holiday buffets, wine tastings, and themed dinners. Check our event calendar for upcoming offerings.
